ENDANGERED SPECIES

en·dan·gered spe·cies

a species of animal or plant that is seriously at risk of extinction.



OUR ADOVOCACY

   SaveMySpecies.org is an online social advocacy movement that aims to alert and educate people about the large number of endangered species. We hope to provide information about the species of flora and fauna that are at risk of being extinct to raise awareness of people as well as to raise funds to contribute to the conservation of these species.

   As of now there are 41,415 species on the IUCN Red List, and 16,306 of them are endangered species threatened with extinction. This is up from 16,118 last year. This includes both endangered animals and endangered plants.

   Our organization serves as a voice to these species. We hope to lower these numbers for the years to come.
